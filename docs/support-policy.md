# Support And Refund Policy

## Effective Date
- Effective from: March 4, 2026

## Support Contact
- Primary support channel: `support@terminal-quest.com`
- Required ticket fields:
  - Purchase channel/order ID
  - Game version
  - OS and terminal environment
  - Reproduction steps
  - Crash log or screenshot (if available)

## Response And Patch SLA
- `P0` (service-blocking, data loss, save corruption):
  - First response: within 24 hours
  - Mitigation patch target: within 48 hours
- `P1` (major progression blocker, frequent crash):
  - First response: within 1 business day
  - Patch target: within 5 business days
- `P2` (minor bug, balancing issue, typo):
  - First response: within 3 business days
  - Patch target: next scheduled patch window

## Refund Policy
- Full refund is supported when all conditions are met:
  - Request is made within 14 days of purchase.
  - Total playtime is under 2 hours.
  - No abuse pattern (repeated purchase/refund cycles).
- If storefront policy is stricter, storefront policy takes precedence.
- If storefront policy is more flexible, this policy is still honored as a minimum standard.

## Known Limitations
- Save files modified manually by users are out of warranty scope.
- Issues caused by unsupported runtime environments may be handled best-effort.

## Telemetry Privacy
- Telemetry is disabled by default and only collected when user opt-in is enabled.
- Only non-PII progression metrics are recorded.
- Telemetry output is stored locally at `telemetry/events.ndjson`.

## Escalation
- For unresolved `P0/P1` issues, escalation owner is the release manager on duty.
- Incident summary and patch ETA are posted in release notes/changelog.
